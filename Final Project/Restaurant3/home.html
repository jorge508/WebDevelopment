Welcome!

Northland is your home for high quality farm fresh angus beef. We are a family owned and operated eatery founded by the Jorgenson family in 1985. Enjoy one of our quarter pound patties grilled to perfection amongst a fresh compilation of condiments, served between two of our signature toasted Northland sesame seed buns. Add a side of our famous hand-cut sea salted french fries to make the perfect meal for you and your family. Don't forget about dessert!
