Welcome!

Northland is your home for high quality farm fresh angus beef. We are a family owned and operated eatery founded by the Jorgenson family in 1985. Enjoy one of our quarter pound patties grilled to perfection amongst a fresh compilation of condiments, served between two of our signature toasted Northland brioche buns. Add a side of our famous hand-cut sea salted french fries to make the perfect meal for you and your family. Don't forget about dessert! We offer 15 different flavors of shakes and malts hand battered to perfection. Located at the corner of walk and don't walk
